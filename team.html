M S Arun (Chairman)
Mr. M S Arun, Chairman, Galilee Intelligent Logistics Technologies Private Limited (GILT) is a highly accomplished Logistics Professional.  Arun is an expert in the field of logistics and security with a great deal of experience in shipping, Container Freight Station and road transport logistics.

Currently he is also Vice President for Chandra CONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS & Terminal Operators Private Limited.  The company was taken over by Team Global Logistics Pvt Ltd in January 2020. In The first month of managing his division, despite the Covid-19 lockdown, Chandra CONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS experienced substantial growth under Arun’s leadership.  Twenty Foot Equivalent Unit (TEUs) increased substantially, impacting the bottom line of the business within a month of his taking over.

Mr. Arun was CEO of NDR Infrastructure Pvt Ltd in Chennai from 2015 to 2019 All cargo Logistics Ltd., in Chennai. Before that, he was Sr. General Manager – CONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS & Inland Container Depots (INLAND CONTAINER DEPOs) Network from 2006 to 2015.  He also held multiple senior level positions with the South India Corporation (Agencies) and SICAL Distripaks.

In addition to his professional roles, Mr. Arun is Chairman of the Chennai Chapter of National Association of Container Freight Stations (NACONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS) from 2012. Key achievements as Chairman of NACONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS, Chennai Chapter include making NACONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS pro-active in Chennai with excellent relations in Customs Commissionerate, Chennai, and in CBIC, Delhi.  He has also played a key role of the prime movers in introducing system driven documentation in Chennai, which is being implemented in all custom houses in India.

Arun and the NACONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS team also introduced RFID for transport vehicles, to ensure that only genuine trailers carry containers for CONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS. This has been lauded by Customs who have subsequently utilized it for paperless documentation.  He received the CERTIFICATE OF MERIT from World Customs Organization in 2017 from Chairman, CBEC. He was the first non-Customs person to receive it for implementing the RFID integrated Trailer and Container Tracking with Paperless Documentation at all the container freight Stations and INLAND CONTAINER DEPOs that are part of the Chennai Chapter of NACONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS, all the Ports and Terminals in Chennai.

S. Arun is a resident of Chennai, has a degree in Mechanical Engineering and post Graduate Degree in Management Sciences from Madras University.  Mr. Arun also received an MBA from Syracuse University in the USA.

Other Positions and Volunteer Commitments:
  • President – Madras Stevedores Association – 1996-1997.
  • Board Member of Madras Dock Labor Board – 1985-1990.
  • Committee Member of Stevedores Association from 1984-1996.
  • Served on various committees of Chennai Port Trust from 1985 to 1993.
  • Committee Member of NACONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS (National Association of CONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONSs) from 2000 to 2004.
  • Served on various committees of Chennai Customs as a representative of NACONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS. Executive Committee Member of NACONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS since 2010.
  • Chairman, NACONTAINER FREIGHT STATIONS – Chennai Chapter, since September 2012
