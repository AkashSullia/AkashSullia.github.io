Eye-Seal
One of our flagship solutions, the Eye-Seal is a smart internal container breach detection system. This Internationally patented solution is developed in collaboration with our US based OEM partner, Eye-Seal, USA, the pioneers in security systems.
  • IoT sensor to detect and alert exporters with time and location details on container breach through door opening, temperature and humidity variation
  • Works in over 180 countries with the same SIM card
  • No need for direct line-of-sight to satellites to determine location
  • Verifiable immediate & schedule alert report (VISAR) history that is equivalent to a survey report or extensive investigative efforts
  • Fully connected DIGITAL device
  • Reliable & tamper-proof internally installed device
  • Provides a detailed end-of-voyage report totalling all breaches and internal environmental variations of the container.
